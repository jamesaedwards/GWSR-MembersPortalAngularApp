Payment Methods

Please select how you would like to pay for your membership. On selecting a payment method, we'll then send you to our payment provider to process your payment.

Direct Debit - with auto-renewal

This option is the easiest and most convienent way to pay for your membership, and allows for automatic renewal of your membership when the time comes so you never miss out on membership benefits (you can opt out of auto-renewal at any time). It also helps the trust as it keeps our administration costs down.

Direct Debit payments are handled by GoCardless. You will need your bank account details, and most banks will require you to authenticate and approve the setting up of direct debit through their online banking service.

Credit/Debit Card - One-off

Pay for your membership with your credit or debit card. Card payments are securly handled by our payment provider Stripe.
