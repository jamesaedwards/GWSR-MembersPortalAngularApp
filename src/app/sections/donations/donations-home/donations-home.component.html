Donations to the Gloucestershire Warwickshire Railway Trust help us through Grant Aid to achieve our aim to support the Gloucestershire Warwickshire Steam Railway as a “Living Museum for the benefit of the public”. All donations go towards to achieving our aim and are not used towards the day to day running expenses or general maintenance.
